OUR STORY

Rooted in Texas,
Focused on Excellence.

Bulletproof Floors started with a simple mission: to provide homeowners in West Texas with a contractor they could actually trust. We saw too many "1-day" floor guys cutting corners with acid etching and cheap paint.

We decided to do it differently. We invested in industrial-grade diamond grinders, partnered with top material manufacturers, and trained our custom crews to treat every home like their own.

Today, we are proud to be the region's highest-rated coating specialists.
