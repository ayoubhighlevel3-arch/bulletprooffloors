Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the installation take?
Most residential garages (up to 800 sq ft) are completed in just ONE DAY. larger projects or extensive moisture mitigation may require a second day.
When can I drive on my new floor?
You can walk on the floor 4-6 hours after completion. We recommend waiting 24 hours before parking vehicles or moving heavy items back in.
Is the floor slippery when wet?
Safety is a priority. Our flake system provides a natural orange-peel texture that offers excellent grip. For areas around pools or steep inclines, we can add an extra anti-slip aggregate to the topcoat.
Why is this better than a DIY kit?
Three reasons: Prep, Product, and Professionals. We diamond-grind the concrete (DIY kits use acid), use industrial grade polyaspartic (DIY is water-based epoxy), and apply it thick. Our floors don't peel.
What if my concrete has cracks or pits?
We repair all minor cracks, pits, and spalling as part of our standard prep process using specialized concrete menders. The final floor will be smooth and seamless.
