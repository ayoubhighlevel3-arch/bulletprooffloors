The Bulletproof Standard

Our 6-Step Installation Process

We don't skip steps. From heavy-duty grinding to our final protective topcoat, our rigorous process ensures a bond that lasts a lifetime.

Phase 01

Diamond Grinding

Prep is everything. We use industrial planetary grinders with diamond tooling to mechanically abrade the concrete surface. This opens the pores, removes weak top layers ("laitance"), and ensures the coating creates an unbreakable mechanical bond. We also attach industrial HEPA vacuums to keep dust to a minimum.

Phase 02

Crack & Pit Repair

We never coat over problems. We "chase" cracks and fill them with a specialized rapid-cure mender that cures stronger than the concrete itself. Pits and spalling are smoothed out to create a seamless, perfect canvas for your new floor.

Phase 03

Base Coat Application

We apply a 100% solids specialized Polyurea base coat. Unlike DIY epoxy, this wicks deep into the open concrete pores, creating a foundational grip that resists peeling and hot tire pickup.

Phase 04

Flake Broadcast

Immediately after the base coat, we broadcast vinyl chips (flakes) into the wet coating until "refusal"—meaning the floor is 100% covered. This provides the decorative look, texture, and added durability/thickness to the system.

Phase 05

Scrape & Vacuum

Once the base coat cures (which is fast!), we scrape the floor with a flat blade to remove any sharp, vertical-standing flakes. We then vacuum up the excess loose flakes. This step is crucial for ensuring the floor is comfortable to walk on while maintaining slip resistance.

Phase 06

Polyaspartic Topcoat

The shield. We apply a clear, UV-stable Polyaspartic topcoat. This layer protects against chemicals, oil spills, abrasion, and road salts. It also amplifies the colors of the flakes and provides a showroom-finish sheen that won't yellow over time.
